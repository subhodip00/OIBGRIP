Brief Biography:

Born Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro on February 5 1985 to Maria Dolores dos Santos Aveiro and Jose Dinis Aveiro. He was named Ronaldo after former U.S President & actor Ronald Reagan.

Cristiano Ronaldo lived in poverty as a child and at the age of 14 he decided to completely focus on Football. He was signed to Sporting CP as a child in his hometown of Madeira - Portugal. During his time at Sporting he showed tremendous potential. He even caught the eye of major clubs like Arsenal and Liverpool. But he was signed by Manchester United instead.

Sir Alex Ferguson was impressed with Cristiano Ronaldo's performance in a friendly match in Lisbon and in 2003 he was transferred for Â£12.24 Million. After the signing he was given the no.7 jersey. No.7 represented the likes of George best, Eric Cantona and David Beckham. He initially wanted the no.28 because of the pressure of no.7 jersey. But he lived up to the greats of no.7. Cristiano Ronaldo took on the role of David Beckham. He became a free kick specialist at Manchester United. He won all the major trophies available with Manchester United such as the English Premier League, The FA Cup, Community Shield, Capital One Cup, UEFA Champions League, UEFA Super Cup and The FIFA Club World Cup. He also won personal awards such as European Golden Shoe and Ballon D'or before moving on to Real Madrid in 2009 where he also tremendiously won a lot of team and individual trophies.

He also represents the Portuguese National team whom he led to European glory in 2016 and he currently plies his trade in Italy with Juventus Fc.
